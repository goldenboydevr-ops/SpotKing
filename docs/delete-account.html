DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

Last updated: June 10, 2026

You can request deletion of your SpotKing account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what gets deleted.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

HOW TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

OPTION 1 — IN THE APP (Instant)

STEP 1

Open SpotKing and tap the Me tab at the bottom of the screen.

STEP 2

Scroll down and tap Settings.

STEP 3

Tap Delete Account at the bottom of the Settings screen.

STEP 4

Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and data will be permanently removed immediately.

OPTION 2 — BY EMAIL

Send a deletion request to support@spotking.app from the email address associated with your account. Include "Delete my account" in the subject line. We will process your request within 7 days.

WHAT GETS DELETED

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email address, password)
  • Your profile (username, avatar, bio)
  • All spots you have added
  • All reviews you have written
  • All photos and videos you have uploaded
  • All votes you have cast
  • Your block list and any reports you have submitted

WHAT IS RETAINED

After deletion, the following may be retained for a limited period:

  • Aggregated, anonymised analytics — retained for up to 90 days for product improvement purposes. This data cannot be linked back to you.
  • Legal compliance records — if we are required by law to retain certain information, we will do so for the minimum period required.

SUBSCRIPTION DATA

If you have an active SpotKing Pro subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Please cancel your subscription through Google Play before deleting your account:

Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → SpotKing → Cancel
