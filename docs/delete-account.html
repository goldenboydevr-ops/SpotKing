DELETE ACCOUNT & DATA REQUESTS

Last updated: June 10, 2026

You have full control over your SpotKing data. This page covers two options: deleting your entire account, or requesting deletion of specific data without closing your account.

OPTION A — DELETE YOUR ENTIRE ACCOUNT

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

IN THE APP (Instant)

STEP 1

Open SpotKing and tap the Me tab at the bottom of the screen.

STEP 2

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.

STEP 3

Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed immediately.

BY EMAIL

Email support@spotking.app with the subject "Delete my account" from the address linked to your account. We will process the request within 7 days.

OPTION B — DELETE SPECIFIC DATA (WITHOUT CLOSING YOUR ACCOUNT)

You can request deletion of specific content without deleting your entire account. We will process these requests within 7 days.

Email support@spotking.app with the subject "Data deletion request" and specify what you'd like removed:

YOU CAN REQUEST REMOVAL OFWHAT STAYS

Please include your username or registered email address so we can identify your account.

SUBSCRIPTION REMINDER

Deleting your account or data does not automatically cancel an active SpotKing Pro subscription. Please cancel via Google Play first:

Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → SpotKing → Cancel
