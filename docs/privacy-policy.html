SPOTKING

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 9, 2026

SpotKing ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SpotKing mobile application (the "App"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address, chosen username, and encrypted password (managed by Supabase Auth).
  • Location data: We request your device location to show nearby spots on the map and in Explore. Location is used only while the app is in use and is never stored on our servers.
  • User-generated content: Spots you add (name, description, coordinates, type, heat level), reviews you write, and photos or videos you upload are stored in our database and may be visible to other users.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymised crash reports and performance data to improve the app.
  • Purchase information: Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and operate the SpotKing service
  • To display your username and content to other users
  • To process subscription purchases and verify entitlements
  • To show nearby spots and undiscovered locations on the map
  • To respond to support requests

SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase – database, authentication, and file storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • RevenueCat – subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Google Maps Platform – map display and nearby place search (policies.google.com/privacy)

USER-GENERATED CONTENT

Spots, reviews, and media you post are visible to all SpotKing users. Do not include personal or sensitive information in public content. You can request deletion of your content by contacting us.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

SpotKing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Contact us at the address below to exercise these rights.

SECURITY

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase's row-level security policies.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
